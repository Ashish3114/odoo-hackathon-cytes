# EcoSphere ESG Management Platform
### Odoo Hackathon 2026 Submission

A custom Odoo 18 module that helps organizations track and manage their Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) performance across departments.

## Problem Statement
Build an ESG Management Platform that enables organizations to measure, manage, and improve their Environmental, Social, and Governance performance, integrating operational data, employee participation, and compliance activities into a unified system with gamification.

## What's Implemented

### Environmental
- **Departments** — organizational units with head, employee count, and ESG tracking
- **Carbon Transactions** — track emissions by category (Purchase, Manufacturing, Expense, Fleet), linked to department and date
- **Emission Factors** — configurable carbon values used for emission calculations (per km, kWh, liters)

### Governance & Categorization
- **Categories** — shared classification system used across CSR activities and gamification challenges

### Gamification
- **Badges** — employee achievements with unlock rules
- **Rewards** — redeemable incentives with points required and stock tracking
